The History of Lyman's Lounge

Originally built by eclectic jazz musician Lyman Hendrickson back in 1938 Lyman's Lounge has been serving great drinks and good times for over 70 years. Our long history of welcoming all types has made us a local landmark. Meant to be a safe haven for multiracial couples and foreigners, Lymans has provided safe haven for a nights out for all types.

After a disastourus fire in 1998 in which half the historic building had burnt down Lymans recieved a generous anonymous donation to rebuild. A grand reopening in 2004 led to a grand expansion of what lymans was all about.

Now serving a resurgent community Lyman's Lounge is now a landmark location for jazz musicians and fans of relaxing nights out.
